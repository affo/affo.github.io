Lorenzo Affetti

lorenzo.affetti@polimi.it

Passionate and enthusiastic PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano (IT) exploring the data processing field.

I am currently investigating the integration between stream processors and databases in the data processing stacks. I proposed a model that embeds the state kept in the database into the internal state of stream processing operators. The model enables consistency guarantees on the state both upon external querying and upon internal updates.
